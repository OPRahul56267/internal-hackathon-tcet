Augmented Reality Experience

Scan the QR code or use your camera to access immersive AR content at cultural sites.

How It Works

  1. Point your device at the QR code located at the heritage site.
  2. Experience 3D models, animations, and stories about the site.
  3. Engage with the content by interacting with it through your device.

Examples of AR Experiences

  • 3D Models of Historical Figures
  • Interactive Historical Reenactments
  • Augmented Narratives with Audio
